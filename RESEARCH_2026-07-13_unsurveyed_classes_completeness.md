## The patent/R&D survivorship-PASS adjudication (the one class that clears wall 1)

Patent/R&D is the one class where the SURVIVORSHIP wall is genuinely passable: USPTO PatentsView
(1976–present) + NBER (1963–1999) are FREE and PERMANENT and carry the full historical grant record
including patents of firms later delisted — the same reason SEC EDGAR companyfacts worked for the GP/A
run. It does NOT die on wall 1. It dies on the other two IN CONJUNCTION:
- **Wall 2 (retail-access) SPLIT, fails on the tradable side.** Raw patent counts/citations are free —
  but the TRADABLE innovative-efficiency signal (HHL 2013) needs the R&D-capital denominator from
  Compustat fundamentals, which is institutional-grade (the app has only free SEC EDGAR companyfacts;
  EODHD fundamentals are PLAN-GATED at $29.99; R&D-capital reconstruction from raw XBRL is a heavy
  build, not a field). The KPSS patent-text importance data IS free (GitHub, 1836–2022) but KPSS
  validate it on contemporaneous market value + forward citations + productivity, NOT as a
  cross-sectional return signal — using it so is un-validated repurposing, not a documented edge.
- **Wall 3 (residual size) fails identically to GP/A.** After the 58% haircut the long leg sits at
  ~1.4%/yr — the same order of magnitude as GP/A's measured ~0.5-1%/yr, which the survivorship-free
  ablation already returned NULL (best DSR 0.326). Patent/R&D IS quality's innovation cousin (the
  tradable IE strand routes through the R&D-capital denominator = a profitability/quality-adjacent
  construct); the quality family's current-era ≈null is already measured. A residual this size cannot
  clear DSR>0.95.
**Judgment: passes survivorship but dies on incremental-to-quality + tiny-residual, exactly like GP/A —
REFUSE stands.** The survivorship PASS is one of three walls; the class is walled on the other two.

## What this survey did NOT establish

- **Institutional-data-purchase unblocks were NOT tested.** Every "DATA-INACCESSIBLE" verdict is
  conditional on RETAIL-tier access. If the owner buys institutional feeds — Revelio $85K/yr (hiring),
  a satellite/card/web-traffic panel ($100K+/yr), or Compustat fundamentals for the patent-IE
  denominator — wall 2/3 change for those classes (though the OOS-decay of supply-chain and the
  look-ahead nature of ESG-vendor history do NOT change with a purchase). These are owner
  data-purchase questions, not surveyed here.
- **Non-US scope.** Judged for the US-first / Saudi-first tradable universe; post-pub decay is
  strongest in the US (Jacobs-Müller) so a non-US application is untested.
- **NLP/reconstruction builds were assessed as heavy, not attempted.** Supply-chain (EDGAR 10-K
  customer NLP) and patent-text are RECONSTRUCTABLE but the build cost is disproportionate to an
  effect already dead OOS (supply-chain) or under-the-bar (patent) — a judgment, not a measurement.
- **Not a disproof of any academic anomaly.** Each in-sample effect is taken as real; the refuse is a
  retail-accessibility + survivorship + cost + decay verdict.
