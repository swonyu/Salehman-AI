## Verified research-backed decisions (confidence labels = adversarial multi-vote)
- **Half-Kelly (0.5√ó) is optimal** ‚Äî 75% of max growth, ruin-of-halving 1/2‚Üí1/8 (Thorp/MacLean-Ziemba; 3-0). App uses 0.5√ó. Do NOT raise it. ‚Üí no-change + iter7 Kelly guardrail.
- **Sizing > signal quality** ‚Äî "worse model + better sizing beats better model + worse sizing" (2-1). ‚Üí ITER1 calibrated win-prob ‚Üí half-Kelly = highest-leverage.
- **maxWeight cap (0.20) needed** ‚Äî raw Kelly can exceed 1 (leverage); 0.5√ó + cap keeps it unleveraged (2-1).

- **Calibration for small-N** ‚Äî isotonic binning unreliable < ~1000 (Niculescu-Mizil & Caruana, Platt-vs-isotonic crossover); Platt below that (Alasalmi 2020). Beta-3param is a stronger *candidate* (quant_engine_II, CONDITIONAL). ‚Üí ITER4 (Platt, isotonicMinSamples=1000). OPEN #2: Platt path is plain MLE not conservative ‚Üí ITER7 ridge-logistic (reconcile w/ Beta-candidate-selector).
- **52-week-high proximity** ‚Äî proximity attenuates crash risk / carries momentum (Byun & Jeon 2023). ‚Üí ITER5 (continuous, long-side, regime-gated).
